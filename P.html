Toyota Harrier

About

The 2017 Toyota Harrier, also known as the Lexus NX in some markets, is a stylish and comfortable SUV known for its refined driving experience and premium features. It comes in both gasoline and hybrid versions, offering a choice between performance and fuel efficiency.

Key Features and Details: Design: The 2017 Harrier features a sleek and modern design, with available features like a Modista body kit for an even sportier look. Engine Options: It offers both a 2.0-liter turbocharged engine (228 horsepower) and a 2.5-liter hybrid engine, providing a balance of power and fuel economy. Interior: The interior is known for its quality, with available features like leather seats, a panoramic sunroof, and a JBL sound system. Technology: The 2017 Harrier includes features like Toyota Safety Sense (with features like lane departure alert and pre-collision system), an electronic tailgate, and various connectivity options. Driving Experience: It's praised for its comfortable ride, smooth handling, and quiet cabin, making it a pleasant vehicle for both city driving and longer journeys. Other Notable Features: Some models come with features like LED headlights, an active front, and a rear spoiler. Hybrid Efficiency: The hybrid version offers impressive fuel efficiency, with a combined consumption as low as 5.0 to 5.8 liters per 100 kilometers. Specific Models and Grades: Elegance: A common grade, available with a 2.0-liter engine and CVT transmission. Premium: Offers enhanced features and luxury compared to the Elegance grade. Progress: Features like a JBL sound system, panoramic view monitor, and pre-crash safety system. Turbocharged: A unique model with a body kit and a more powerful 2.0-liter turbocharged engine. Potential Considerations: Body Kit: The Modista body kit, while adding to the sporty look, can impact ground clearance. Engine Oil Consumption: Some users have reported issues with engine oil consumption in certain conditions, particularly with the 2.0-liter engine. Overall: The 2017 Toyota Harrier is a well-regarded SUV that offers a blend of style, comfort, and technology. Its available hybrid powertrain and various trim levels provide options for different preferences and needs
